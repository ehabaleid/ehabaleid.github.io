Fuel Efficiency of ICE(Internal Combustion Engine) Vehicles

In this interactive slideshow we will explore the fuel economy of ICE(internal combustion engine) vehicles vs BEV(battery electric vehicles).
In this first scene you can explore the average city/highway/combined MPG(miles per gallon) of ICE vehicles by vehicle classification.
Hover over a vehicle bar to view the combined MPG in a tooltip

Select a radio button to see the difference between city and highway MPG for each vehicle class

The fuel economy data for this visualization is based off 2022 model year vehicles sourced from the US Department of Energy
